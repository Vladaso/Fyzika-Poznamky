$v=v_0+-at$
$a = \frac{v-v_0}{t}$
$a = \frac{-20 \frac{m}{s}}{10s}$
$a = -2 m/s^2$
$s=v_0t+-\frac{1}{2}at^2$
$s=20 \frac{m}{s}*10s - \frac{1}{2}2m/s^{2}*10s^2$
